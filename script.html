Script

Version: 1.1 (Last updated: June 13, 2018)

The unique script for Ta'agra is now available in a variety of fonts that you can download and use on your computer. Using these fonts requires a few extra steps, however. Since Ta'agra's writing system is different from what most programs are used to, be sure to check out the installation instructions or our tutorial video to get started.

Ta'agra Script Sample

Below is a large sample of Ta'agra script, to give an example of how it looks when a substantial amount is written together. What is written below are S'rathra's responses to Cyrus spoken in Ta'agra. You can find the link to the video and the analysis of the conversation on the Source Documents page.

Krimir, Sairus.
Etofor wakay jer oku dorr.
Bali jihatta ra tataami.
Shijoh.
Renriji in tenurr.
Hokoh wo Imperialjah.
Iko pofamer rotot velk.
An traajir.
S'rathra kor ko derodoh Hammerfell.
Bo shay irot.
S'rathra woorika.
Mukoh Duadeen.
S'rathra eks. S'rathra eks skra'il.
Roliter dek ka udurr.
Teport teko'i.

Sri Duadeen jah.
Epako jah ashrik.
Pur S'rathra kador aaliter bekoh?
